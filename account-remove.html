📋 How to submit a deletion request

Follow these steps to permanently delete your Pop Song Chord Book account:

1
Scroll down to the "Submit deletion request" form on this page.
2
Enter the email address you used to sign in to Pop Song Chord Book.
3
Check the confirmation box and click "Request Account Deletion". This opens your email app pre-filled with a deletion request.
4
Send the email. You will receive a confirmation reply within 48 hours.
5
Your account and data are fully deleted within 30 days of your confirmed request.

Alternatively, you can email info@popsongchordbook.com directly with the subject Account Deletion Request.

🗄️ Data deleted & data retained

Permanently deleted

  • Your Firebase login account & credentials
  • Saved song favourites & personal preferences
  • Student / teacher profile data (if applicable)
  • Personal notes and customisations
  • Usage history linked to your account

Temporarily retained after deletion

  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data — retained up to 90 days, then auto-deleted. This data cannot be linked back to you.
  • Email correspondence sent to info@popsongchordbook.com — retained for up to 1 year for support record purposes, then permanently deleted.
⏳ All personal data deleted within 30 days
