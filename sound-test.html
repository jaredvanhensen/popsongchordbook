Lab Notes:

  • Attack: Fade-in time. Guitar/Ukulele use extremely short values.
  • Decay/Sustain: Decay is how fast the initial pluck drops to the sustain level.
  • Release: How long the sound lingers after stopping. Higher values = longer sustain feel.
  • Strum Stagger (Internal): When playing chords, the notes are slightly staggered (30-50ms) to simulate a pick.
