Introducing: Song Map

A professional overview of your song structure

The Song Map provides a comprehensive bird's-eye view of your entire song structure, allowing you to see the big picture at a glance. Label sections like Intro, Verse, and Chorus with distinct colors, making it easy to identify the progression of the track. While playing along, the active chord is highlighted and moves in real-time, so you always know exactly where you are in the arrangement. This feature also offers the ability to jump instantly to any part of the song by simply clicking on a section. Whether you're rehearsing or performing, the Song Map keeps you perfectly oriented and in control of your music.
